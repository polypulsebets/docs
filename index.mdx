## Understanding Prices

**Prices = Probabilities.**

The price of a share reflects the market's estimated probability of that event happening.

> **Example:**
> If "YES" shares for *PLS hitting $0.0002* are trading at **18 cents**, the market believes there is an **18% chance** of that event occurring.

If you believe the true probability is **higher** than 18%, buying "YES" is a winning strategy.
