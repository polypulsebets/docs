# How to Sign-Up

PolyPulseBets uses **decentralized authentication**. You do not need an email, phone number, or password—just a crypto wallet.

<Note>
  **Need Help?**
  
  We’re available to guide you through the sign-up process via our **[Support Center](/support)**.
</Note>

## Crypto Wallet Sign-Up

PolyPulseBets supports most EVM-compatible crypto wallets, including **MetaMask**, **Rabby**, **Internet Money Wallet**, and others via **WalletConnect**.

**Signing up is instant:**

1.  Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner.
2.  Select your preferred wallet from the list.
3.  **Switch Network** to PulseChain (or Testnet) if prompted.
4.  **Sign the Transaction:** This is a gas-free signature request that simply proves you own the wallet.
5.  **Set Username:** You will be prompted to create a unique display name. This handle is used to track your rank on the **Leaderboard** and identify you in **Market Discussions**.

Once completed, your account is fully active and ready to trade!
