# How to Sign-Up

PolyPulseBets uses decentralized authentication. You do not need an email or password—just a crypto wallet.

<Note>
**Need Help?** We’re available to guide you through the sign-up process on [Discord](#).
</Note>

## Crypto Wallet Sign-Up

PolyPulseBets supports most EVM-compatible crypto wallets, including **MetaMask**, **Rabby**, **Internet Money Wallet**, and others via **WalletConnect**.

Signing up is instant:

1.  Click the **Connect Wallet** button in the top right corner.
2.  Select your preferred wallet.
3.  **Switch Network** to PulseChain if prompted.
4.  Sign the transaction (this is free and verifies you own the wallet).
5.  **Set Username:** You will be asked to create a unique display name. This username is used to track your rank on the **Leaderboard** and identify you in **Market Discussions**.

Once completed, your account is fully active!
