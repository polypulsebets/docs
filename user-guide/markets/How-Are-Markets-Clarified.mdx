# Market Clarifications

Sometimes, real-world events are messy. A "Clarification" is an official update to the market rules to resolve ambiguity **before** the market closes.

## When does this happen?
* **Example:** A market asks *"Who will win the election?"* but one candidate dies before the election.
* **Action:** A clarification might be issued stating: *"If a candidate withdraws or passes away, bets on them will resolve to NO."*
