## Close-Only Jurisdictions

Some jurisdictions (like Singapore, Poland, Thailand, and Taiwan) may have a "close-only" or "redemption-only" status.
* ✅ **Allowed:** Redeeming winnings after a market resolves.
* ❌ **Restricted:** Opening new positions (placing new bets).

## Why These Restrictions?

Geographic restrictions are implemented to ensure compliance with:
1.  **International Sanctions:** OFAC and other global embargoes.
2.  **Local Regulations:** Gambling and prediction market laws specific to your jurisdiction.
3.  **AML/KYC:** Anti-money laundering requirements.
