# Contact Us

We are available to help you with technical issues, deposit problems, or general questions.

## Channels

* **Website Button:** The fastest way to reach us is to click the **Support Button** located on the main page of the website.
* **Discord:** Join our [Community Discord](#) and open a ticket in `#support`.
* **Email:** Send details to [hello@polypulsebets.com](mailto:hello@polypulsebets.com).

<Warning>
**Safety Tip:** Support will **NEVER** ask for your Seed Phrase or Private Key. If someone asks for this, they are a scammer.
</Warning>
